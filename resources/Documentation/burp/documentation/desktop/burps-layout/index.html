Customizing Burp's layout

You have lots of options for customizing the layout of Burp's user interface, to suit your preferences. You can customize the top-level tabs, and some tables for different tools. For example, you can:

  • Change the order of content.
  • Hide content.
  • Detach top-level tabs. This enables you to open a tab, or groups of tabs, in a new window. You can open and arrange windows to suit your work style.

You can also customize the UI settings, to change Burp's appearance.
