Annotating Organizer items

You can add notes to, highlight, and update the status of Organizer items. This enables you to:

  • Track your workflow more easily.
  • Flag messages for further investigation.
  • Capture your thoughts for later attention.

To add notes to an item, double-click in the Notes column. The Notes panel opens.

Note

If you added notes in another Burp tool, these are copied into Organizer. In addition, if you send a message from Organizer to a tool that uses notes, your comments are copied across from Organizer.

To highlight an item, click the # column and choose a color from the drop-down menu.

To update the status of an item, click the Status column and select a status from the drop-down menu. You can choose from:

  • New
  • In progress
  • Postponed
  • Done
  • Ignored
